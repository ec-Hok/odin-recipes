Air Fryer French Fries

A plate of French Fries made with Air Fryer

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Kosher Salt

How to Make French Fries in the Air Fryer

  1. Cut the Potatoes

    2. Use a sharp knife to cut each peeled potato into slices, then sticks.

  2. Soak and Drain the Potatoes

    3. Soak the potato sticks in a bowl of cold water for about 5 minutes. Drain, then pour boiling water over the potatoes. Let sit for about 10 minutes. Drain and dry the potatoes.

  3. Season the French Fries

    Allow the potato sticks to cool, then transfer to a mixing bowl. Toss them with oil and season with cayenne Pepper.

  4. Cook in Air Fryer

    5. Air frythe potato sticks in a double layer in the fryer basket. Cook for 15 minutes, toss, then cook for 10 more minutes. Season with salt.
